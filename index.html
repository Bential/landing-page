The military lycées de la Défense are establishments providing general and technological academic education, from middle school to preparatory classes for major military and civilian schools, in accordance with ParcourSup criteria.

These are boarding schools allowing students to have a stable education despite the mobility of their parents and to receive quality education. The pedagogy, supervision and the work environment promote their development, while respecting the values of camaraderie, a taste for effort and accountability.
